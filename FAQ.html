Frequently Asked Questions

Who is Aynu?

Aynu, also known as AynuDaDev, is an indie game developer and Python programmer who creates unique, experimental games in unexpected genres.

Is Aynu the same as Aynu Labs?

No. Aynu is an individual indie game developer, whereas Aynu Labs is a separate company that publishes educational apps like Aynu Kids on Google Play.

What kind of games do you make?

I focus on creative, experimental games with my own distinct style. Some notable projects include Teleporting Tesseracts, BoomLoop, LOL Machine 3000, Smack N' Cheese, and Artificial Intellipong.

Where can I play your games?

My games are available on itch.io and my code can be found on GitHub.

Do you work with other developers or participate in game jams?

Yes, I participate in indie game development communities like Alakajam! and I have not worked with other Dev(s) till date.
